About — one culture, two disciplines

Designers at heart. From software to cinema.

One design culture, two disciplines, fifteen-plus years. The engineers think like designers. The directors do too. That's the whole trick.

The studio happened to us

POWER SHIFTER started as a digital product studio and stayed one — design-led from the first hire, never a dev shop, building platforms and products for brands like TELUS, Energizer, and lululemon. The founder came up the other way around: film and television, then commercial production alongside Ridley Scott's Black Dog Films and ILM, then design.

That history sat quietly for fifteen years. Then generative AI made it relevant overnight. Most of the field is still making spec spots. We're making the real thing, on six-figure client budgets — a second discipline we didn't plan, staffed by people who've been making pictures for decades. We didn't chase the trend. The trend circled back to where we started.

So now there are two doors: software and cinema. Behind both, the same design culture and the same rule — the work and the place it lives, made in the same room.
