## Block 2 — The story

_Two-column band: H2 left, prose right. Origin narrative — design-led from day one, generative AI surfacing thirty years of latent production lineage, "two doors / same room" resolution._

**H2:** The studio happened to us

**BODY:** POWER SHIFTER started as a digital product studio and stayed one — design-led from the first hire, never a dev shop, building platforms and products for brands like TELUS, Energizer, and lululemon. The founder came up the other way around: film and television, then commercial production alongside Ridley Scott's Black Dog Films and ILM, then design.

**BODY:** That history sat quietly for fifteen years. Then generative AI made it relevant overnight. What began as one person's experiments became **half a million dollars of booked film work in a year** — and a second practice we didn't plan, staffed by people who've been making pictures for decades. We didn't chase the trend. The trend circled back to where we started.

**BODY:** So now there are two doors: software and cinema. Behind both, the same design culture and the same rule — **the work and the place it lives, made in the same room.**
